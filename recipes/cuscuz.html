Couscous

Home

The food so nice, they named it twice!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (200g) uncooked couscous
  • 1½ cups (360ml) boiling water or vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (or butter)
  • ½ tsp salt

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the couscous, olive oil, and salt. Pour the boiling water or broth over the couscous. Immediately cover the bowl with a lid or plate. Let it steam for 5 minutes until the liquid is absorbed.
  2. Use a fork to gently fluff the couscous, breaking up any clumps.
  3. In a pan, sauté diced veggies in olive oil until tender (3–4 minutes). Mix into the couscous. Stir in herbs, dried fruit, nuts, or lemon zest for extra flavor and texture.
  4. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt, pepper, or a drizzle of olive oil. Serve warm or at room temperature.
